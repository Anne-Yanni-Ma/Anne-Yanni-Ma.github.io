Brief Bio

I obtained my B.E. degree and M.E. degree in Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU). Currently, I'm a fouth-year Phd student in SYSU, supervised by Prof. Guo. I'm also a visiting student in University of Amsterdam(UvA), and supervised by Prof. Shaodi You. My research interests are in 3D scene understanding. Now I mainly focus on 3D scene graph generation.
